Mr. Biswajit Haridasan, M.Tech (Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University), B.Tech (CUSAT)

Director, Sense Design

Biswajit is a Director at Sense Design (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. with extensive experience in CAD & FEA of Construction & Bulk Material Handling Equipment. Mr. Sambandham Ganesan, MBA (Netherlands), M.Tech (IIT Madras), B.Tech (IIT Madras)

Ganesan is an advisor at Sense Design (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. with 42 years of experience in multi-disciplinary engineering and the mining industry. He holds both a B.Tech and an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Madras and has worked extensively in Port Material Handling Machines, Mining Machinery, and Construction Equipment.
